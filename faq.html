Q: What is the dark staining on the sides of my home?

A: Most likely it is algae, mold or mildew. All of these like to grow on the sides of your home that get the least amount of sun and the highest humidity. These stains like to grow on vinyl siding, treated wood, glass, stone, bricks, etc.

Q: Why don't you list prices on your website?

A: Every house is different. Different sizes, shapes and location can all change the price of a job. To be fair, I would have to take a look at your home and properly evaluate what needs to be done.

Q: What are the benefits of having my house pressure washed?

A: Having your house pressure washed can benefit your home in many ways. Right away you will be able to see a difference in how clean the house will look. The curb appeal of your house will go up because it is looking new again. It is also good for the health of you and your family. What is on the outside of your house can soon be going into the vents and ducts. The best way to stop or slow down this process is to keep the outside of your house mold and mildew free!
